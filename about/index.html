Our Story

Meet Hyun Ja Ahn

For over 20 years, Hyun Ja Ahn has called Hawaii home. After falling in love with the islands' spirit of healing and care, she knew this was the place to share her life's passion — helping people feel confident and beautiful in their own skin.

With specialized training in Brazilian lymphatic drainage, body contouring, and advanced skin care techniques, Hyun Ja has dedicated her career to mastering the art of natural wellness. Every treatment she performs reflects years of hands-on experience and a deep understanding of how the body heals, sculpts, and renews itself.

Whether you're here to sculpt, detoxify, or simply take a moment for yourself, Hyun Ja is honored to be part of your journey. Her warmth, expertise, and genuine care for every client is what makes Slim Life truly special.
