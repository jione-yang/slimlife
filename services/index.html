Signature Treatments

A closer look at our most popular services and what makes them special.

01

Brazilian Lymphatic Drainage Massage

Our signature treatment. Using rhythmic, precise Brazilian techniques, we stimulate your lymphatic system to naturally detoxify and contour your body. Feel lighter, look sculpted, and experience visible results from your very first session.

  • Reduces bloating and water retention
  • Natural body contouring and sculpting
  • Boosts immune system and circulation
  • Supports post-surgical recovery
02

Brazilian Face Lymphatic System

A luxurious facial treatment using gentle lymphatic drainage to reduce puffiness, improve skin tone, and sculpt your facial contours. Walk out with a radiant, lifted glow — no invasive procedures needed.

  • Reduces facial puffiness and dark circles
  • Improves skin elasticity and tone
  • Natural face-lifting effect
03

Radio Frequency & Ultrasonic Cavitation

Harness the power of RF heat energy and ultrasonic waves to target stubborn fat cells and tighten skin. This gentle, non-invasive treatment delivers visible contouring and skin rejuvenation with zero downtime.

  • Targets stubborn fat deposits
  • Tightens and firms loose skin
  • Non-invasive with no downtime
04

Collagen Tornado

Awaken your skin's natural collagen production for firmer, more youthful results. This innovative treatment revitalizes elasticity, smooths texture, and helps reduce the appearance of fine lines and cellulite.

  • Stimulates natural collagen production
  • Improves skin firmness and texture
  • Reduces appearance of cellulite
05

Radio Frequency & Suction Therapy

A powerful pairing of RF technology with vacuum suction to enhance circulation, smooth skin, and break down stubborn fat. This dual-action treatment delivers beautiful contouring results you can see and feel.

  • Enhanced circulation and lymphatic flow
  • Effective cellulite reduction
  • Smoother, firmer skin texture
06

Wood Therapy

An ancient sculpting art using handcrafted wooden tools to reshape your silhouette naturally. Through targeted pressure and rolling movements, wood therapy breaks down stubborn fat and fibrous cellulite for beautifully defined curves.

  • Natural body sculpting and contouring
  • Breaks down stubborn fat deposits
  • Reduces cellulite appearance
07

Post-Op & Pre-Natal Lymph Massage

Specialized lymphatic drainage designed to support women through pregnancy and surgical recovery. Prenatal massage relieves back pain, reduces joint swelling, and improves circulation using safe side-lying positions. Post-op lymphatic drainage uses gentle, rhythmic techniques to reduce swelling, prevent fibrosis, and accelerate healing following procedures like liposuction, tummy tucks, and C-sections.

  • Reduces swelling, bruising, and edema
  • Prevents and breaks down fibrosis and scar tissue
  • Relieves pregnancy-related back pain and joint discomfort
  • Speeds up post-surgical recovery time
